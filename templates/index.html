Iris setosa

It is the bristle-pointed iris.

It is a species of flowering plant in the genus Iris of the family Iridaceae, it belongs the subgenus Limniris and the series Tripetalae.

Iris versicolor

It is also commonly known as the blue flag.

Also known as harlequin blueflag, larger blue flag, northern blue flag,and poison flag, plus other variations of these names, and in Britain and Ireland as purple iris.

Iris virginica

It has the common name Virginia iris.

It is a perennial species of flowering plant, native to eastern North America.

Description

    The iris dataset contains three classes of flowers, Versicolor, Setosa, Virginica, and each class contains 4 features, ‘Sepal length’, ‘Sepal width’, ‘Petal length’, ‘Petal width’. The aim of the iris flower classification is to predict flowers based on their specific features.
